Our Commitment

Sustainability

Performance without compromise. We're dedicated to creating world-class products while protecting our planet.

01

Carbon Neutral by 2025

We've achieved our goal of carbon-neutral manufacturing ahead of schedule. All NIKE products are now made using 100% renewable energy, and we offset any remaining emissions through verified carbon credits.

02

Recycled Materials

75% of our products now use recycled materials. Our FLUX foam is made from recycled ocean plastics, and our mesh uppers use recycled polyester from post-consumer waste.

03

Zero Waste Initiative

By 2027, we aim to eliminate all manufacturing waste. Our circular design program allows customers to return worn shoes for recycling, turning old products into new materials.
