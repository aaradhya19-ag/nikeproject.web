Returns

60-day returns on all orders. Free exchanges. No questions asked.

01

Return Policy

We want you to love your VELOCITY products. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return unworn items within 60 days of delivery for a full refund.

  • Items must be unworn and in original packaging
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Returns are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds issued to original payment method
02

Free Exchanges

Wrong size? We offer free exchanges on all orders. Simply initiate an exchange through your account, and we'll send your new size as soon as we receive your return.

03

How to Return

  1. Log into your account and find your order
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Print your prepaid return label
  4. Pack items securely and drop off at any carrier location
