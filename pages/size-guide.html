How to Measure

  1. Stand on a piece of paper with your heel against a wall
  2. Mark the longest part of your foot on the paper
  3. Measure the distance from the wall to the mark in centimeters
  4. Use the CM column in our size chart to find your size

Fit Tips

  • Measure your feet in the afternoon when they're largest
  • If between sizes, we recommend sizing up
  • Racing shoes run narrow - consider sizing up for wider feet
  • Free exchanges available if your first size doesn't fit
