FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 60-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Visit our Returns page for detailed instructions on how to initiate a return.

How do I find my shoe size?

Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements and fitting recommendations. We also offer free exchanges if your first size doesn't fit perfectly.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary by location. See our Shipping page for details.

How do I care for my NIKE shoes?

Clean with a soft brush and mild soap. Air dry away from direct heat. Avoid machine washing. Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna for installment payments.
