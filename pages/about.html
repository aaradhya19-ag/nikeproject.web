About nike

We exist to help athletes push beyond limits. Every product we create is born from innovation and driven by performance.

01

Our Mission

Founded in 2018, NIKE was born from a simple belief: athletes deserve footwear that matches their ambition. We combine cutting-edge materials science with biomechanical engineering to create products that don't just meet expectations—they shatter them.

Every shoe we design starts with a question: How can we help athletes go faster, farther, and stronger? The answer drives everything we do, from our research labs to our production facilities.

02

Innovation First

Our R&D team includes aerospace engineers, biomechanics experts, and former professional athletes. Together, they develop proprietary technologies like FLUX foam cushioning and our carbon-fiber propulsion plates that have redefined what's possible in athletic footwear.

03

Athlete Tested

We partner with over 500 professional athletes across 12 sports to test and refine every product. Their feedback is invaluable—it ensures that when you lace up a pair of NIKE shoes, you're wearing gear that's been proven at the highest levels of competition.
